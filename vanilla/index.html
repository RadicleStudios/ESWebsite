Vanilla night&day

Vanilla night&day is a Polish luxury nightwear brand founded in 2006. For more than a decade bedrooms and salons have been graced, from Paris, through Rome, Shanghai, and Dubai, to Warsaw. Manufactured using only the finest Polish, Italian, and French fabrics in a renovated and revitalized historic factory in Lodz. Vanilla night&day is in permanent cooperation with the Academy of Fine Arts in Lodz.

Feminine, sexy, and very delicate underwear and loungewear, Vanilla was created for women who desire to feel pampered and distinguished. Each piece is a small work of art incorporating the finest silk, viscose, or beautiful lace elements. Making evenings magical and mornings more energetic, Vanilla night&day above all celebrate femininity.

Kasia

Kasia Pastwa, mathematics graduate, decided to follow her passion and create something unique. In 2006 she founded Vanilla to create unique nightwear for women who didn't just want to put on that stretched-out t-shirt. She wanted to create nightwear whose shape, sewing, and details will highlight a woman's form and make her feel special.

It was her dream that thanks to Vanilla night&day, women everywhere after a hard day's work can still find and celebrate their feminity. Today, this dream is a reality, and Kasia Pastwa urges new ladies each day, Look after yourself and your feminity, even at night.
